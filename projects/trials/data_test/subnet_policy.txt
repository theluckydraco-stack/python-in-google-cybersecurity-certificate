Restricted Subnet Policy - Patient Records Environment

Subnet Name: PR-SEGMENT-01
Approved Range: 10.52.14.0/24 - 10.52.19.0/24
Extended Temporary Range: 10.52.20.0/24

Rules:
1. Only approved employee workstations may appear in the allow list.
2. Contractor access must expire immediately after engagement end date.
3. Suspended or revoked users must be removed from the allow list during the next review cycle.
4. Duplicate IP addresses should not be retained in the final allow list.
5. Invalid IP entries must be reported and excluded.
6. All changes must be auditable.
